Sea Weather System

A Sea Weather Buoy is an oceanic device designed to collect and transmit meteorological and oceanographic data from the sea. It typically consists of sensors to measure parameters like wave height, sea temperature, atmospheric pressure, and wind speed. These buoys play a crucial role in monitoring and forecasting marine weather conditions, aiding navigation and supporting scientific research.
